About Me

I'm Caleb Callihan, a creative developer and marketer who builds ideas that connect people. I design experiences that combine visual storytelling, practical design, and technical precision across interactive projects, campaigns, and digital media.

With experience in marketing, game design, and brand development, I focus on turning creative concepts into clear, effective results. My work explores how emotion and aesthetics can drive engagement and how small details can elevate an experience.

Outside of development, I explore photography, writing, and design to refine my creative voice. Every project is a chance to learn, adapt, and create something meaningful.
