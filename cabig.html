Squarewave

Build your ultimate loadout from 24 unique shots. Break blocks, score points, and climb the global leaderboard in this minimalist arcade shooter.

View on Google Play

Gameplay Overview

In Squarewave, blocks continuously descend from the top of the screen. Destroying them scores points, but if they reach the bottom, the game ends. As your score climbs, blocks gain health and speed, pushing you to adapt and survive. Before each run, select 5 of 24 shots — from missiles and lasers to poisons and chain attacks — to build a personalized deck of abilities.


  • Break descending blocks to score points.
  • Choose 5 of 24 shot types to form your deck.
  • Difficulty increases as you survive longer.
  • The game ends when a block touches the bottom.
